# CryoconiteRTM

Codes to calculate absorption of energy in a cryoconite hole, specifically including a collimated direct beam subject to losses due to specular reflection at the air/water interface and multiple reflections between the hole walls

Currently assumes point at centre of hole is representative of entire hole floor (i.e. if centre is not directly illuminated none of the floor is directly illuminated, if centre of hole is directly illumonated so is the rest of the floor).
